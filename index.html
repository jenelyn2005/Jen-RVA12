How it works

1

Registration

Households, businesses, and charities create a free account. Each role gets a tailored dashboard suited to their needs.

2

Listing

Surplus food items are posted with details — type of food, quantity, and best-by date. Takes under a minute.

3

Claiming & Donating

Community members browse available items and claim them. Businesses can directly offer donations to verified food banks.

4

Coordination

The platform facilitates communication and scheduling for pickups and drop-offs, optimizing routes for efficiency.

5

Impact Tracking

Users see the collective impact of their contributions — food diverted from landfills and the number of meals provided.

Households

Manage your pantry, share surplus food with neighbors, and get recipe ideas for what you have.

Business

Donate surplus stock to verified charities, track your waste, and streamline pickup logistics.

Food Banks

Post real-time needs, receive donation alerts, and optimize your collection routes.
